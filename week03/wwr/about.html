Rafting Adventure Co.

Our mission is to provide world-class outdoor experiences that connect people with nature and each other. We believe that a day on the river is a day well spent, and we strive to make every trip safe, exciting, and memorable for adventurers of all skill levels.

History

Founded in 2005, Rafting Adventure Co. started with just two rafts and a passion for the rapids. Over the last two decades, we have expanded to three different river locations, helping over 50,000 guests experience the thrill of white water while maintaining a perfect safety record.
