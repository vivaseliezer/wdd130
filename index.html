Guatemala

Guatemala is a central American country located south of Mexico, celebrated for its rugged volcanic skylines, thick tropical rainforests, and centuries-old Mayan civilizations.

Its historic capital, Guatemala City, features deep architectural landmarks, while beautiful Antigua displays perfectly preserved Spanish colonial architectures and majestic active mountain views.
